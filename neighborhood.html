About Sandy

Sandy, Utah is located just south of Salt Lake City! It is close to everything you could ever desire. There are plenty of restaraunts you can choose from, along with beautiful parks such as the Dimple Dell Trail. You are 20 minutes away from skiing or snowboarding at some of the finest resorts around. Come on by and visit!
